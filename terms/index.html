Page to JSON browser extension

Page to JSON ("the extension") is a browser extension that exports the data of the web page you are actively viewing into a JSON file, processed entirely on your device. By installing or using the extension, you agree to these terms.

General

The extension is provided "as is", without warranty of any kind, express or implied. You use it at your own risk.

The extension works by reading the content of the page you are viewing when you click Export, optionally fetching additional data from that same page's website over your existing session, and converting it to JSON locally. Because pages change and vary, extraction may be incomplete or imperfect; when the extension cannot confirm its output it flags the result as unverified rather than presenting it as complete.
